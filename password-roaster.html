What the Roaster Checks

The roast isn't random — it's driven by real password analysis. The engine checks:

  • Length — the single biggest factor in password strength
  • Complexity — uppercase, lowercase, digits, symbols
  • Common patterns — "password", "123456", "qwerty", keyboard walks
  • Repeated characters — "aaaaaaa" scores badly, as it should
  • Common words — admin, letmein, welcome, monkey, sunshine

The score combines all factors. Specific weaknesses are called out individually so you know exactly what to fix — or, at minimum, why you're being judged so harshly.
